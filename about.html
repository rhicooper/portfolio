About

Background

Born and raised on the sunny east coast of Australia, my inherent curiosity has taken me around the world and currently has me based in the eclectic and creative city of Bristol, UK.

Pre- UX Design, I have had a varied professional career which has allowed me to develop a broad range of experience on which to draw from. I have worked in marketing (for tourism, events, healthcare and education), event management, EFL teaching, travel sales, hospitality, and even worked on short film productions as a Production Coordinator!

While happy working individually or as part of a team, I truly thrive in an interactive environment with opportunities for real connection. I love working with individuals or communities to research and understand their needs, uncover what challenges they face and develop solutions that enable them to carry out their goal. I have a hands-on approach to life and a fundamental ‘Can-Do’ attitude to draw on when faced with a problem to solve.

Outlook

Above all things I am passionate about learning - be that mastering a new skill, studying a new topic or opening the mind to different ideas, perspectives and cultures. I have experienced living in four different countries with their own unique cultures, travelled extensively (35 countries and counting!) and met with people from all walks to life. My experiences and interactions have helped to shape my world view while ensuring I remain open to exploring new or alternate perspectives and understandings.

The biggest hope I have is for my work to positively benefit the lives of those it may impact!

On weekends I can most often be found wandering around local makers or farmers markets, coffee in hand and tote bag at the ready to be filled with whatever treasures I find. I love to travel when I can and explore new places but am equally happy whiling away the hours snuggled up with a good book or movie.
